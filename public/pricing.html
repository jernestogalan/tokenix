Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to create an account?

No. The free tier works without any account. Just paste text and analyze tokens instantly.

How accurate are the token counts?

For OpenAI models we use tiktoken (exact). For Claude we use the official API. For other models, counts are estimates labeled "Est." so you always know which is which.

Is my data stored?

No. All text is processed in memory and discarded immediately. Nothing is logged, stored, or shared.
