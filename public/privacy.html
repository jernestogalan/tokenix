Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · Beta

Tokenia ("we", "us", "our") operates tokenia.live. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our token counting and cost estimation service.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what is strictly necessary to operate the service:

  • Text you submit for analysis. This is processed in memory only and is not retained, logged, or stored after the API call completes.
  • Files you upload. File content is extracted, analyzed, and immediately discarded. We do not store file contents.
  • Account data (optional). If you create an account, we store your email address and hashed password via Supabase. You can delete your account at any time.
  • Anonymous usage statistics. We may collect aggregate, non-identifiable metrics (e.g. page views, feature usage counts) to improve the service.

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide and improve the token analysis service.
  • To authenticate users if they choose to create an account.
  • To respond to support requests.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Text and File Privacy

Your prompts, documents, and code files are never stored on our servers. All analysis happens in memory during the API request and the content is immediately discarded. We have no access to your text after the response is returned to your browser.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use minimal browser storage:

  • sessionStorage: Your language preference (e.g. tokenia_lang) is stored for the duration of your browser session.
  • We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — for optional user authentication and account management.
  • Railway — our hosting provider. Your IP address may appear in their server logs per standard HTTP logging.
  • Google Fonts — the Inter typeface is loaded from Google's CDN. Google's privacy policy applies to that request.

6. Security

We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plaintext. We follow industry standard practices to protect your account data.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing support@tokenia.live. Since we do not store text inputs, there is no text data to delete beyond what session memory holds.

8. Children

Tokenia is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We will post any changes to this page and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@tokenia.live.

This policy is provided in good faith for a beta product. It will be reviewed by legal counsel prior to commercial launch.
