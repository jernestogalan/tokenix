Beta Privacy Notice

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2025  ·  Last updated: June 2025

Beta notice: Tokenia is currently in private beta. This policy reflects our current minimal data practices. We will notify registered users of any material changes before they take effect.

01 Who we are

Tokenia is an independent developer tool for estimating and reducing token costs when working with large language model APIs. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, or any other AI provider.

For questions about this policy, contact: privacy@tokenia.live

02 What data we collect

Text and files you submit

When you paste text or upload a file for token counting or analysis, that content is processed in memory on our server to perform the calculation. We do not store the full contents of your text or files. Files are discarded immediately after processing; they are never written to permanent storage.

Account data (registered users)

When you create an account we collect:

  • Email address (used for login and product updates)
  • Hashed password (managed by Supabase Auth — we never see your plaintext password)
  • Plan tier (free, pro, or team)
  • Monthly token usage counter (a number — not your actual text)

Usage events (history feature)

When you are signed in and the history feature is enabled, we log metadata about each operation: the event type, approximate token count, estimated cost, the model you selected, your plan at the time, and the filename (if you uploaded a file). We do not log the actual text content.

Server logs

Our server logs standard HTTP request metadata: IP address, timestamp, URL path, HTTP method, and response status code. Logs are retained for up to 30 days for security and debugging purposes.

03 What we do not collect

  • We do not use tracking pixels, third-party analytics (Google Analytics, Mixpanel, etc.), or advertising scripts.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not store or read the text you paste or the files you upload beyond the moment of processing.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking. Session state is managed via a JWT stored in your browser's local storage (Supabase Auth default).

04 How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and enforce your plan's usage limits
  • To display your token usage history (if the feature is enabled and you are signed in)
  • To send critical product or security announcements to registered users
  • To debug errors and maintain the reliability of the service

We will never use your data to train AI models or sell it to data brokers.

05 Data storage and security

Account data and usage metadata are stored in Supabase, a managed PostgreSQL database hosted on AWS. Data at rest is encrypted. All connections are made over HTTPS/TLS.

Our server runs on Railway. We apply row-level security (RLS) policies in Supabase so that each user can only access their own rows — even application-level bugs cannot expose other users' data.

Service role key: The Supabase service role key (which bypasses RLS) is stored only as a server-side environment variable on Railway. It is never exposed to the browser or included in any public API response.

06 Your rights

You may at any time:

  • Request your data — email us and we will provide a JSON export of your profile and usage events.
  • Delete your account — email us and we will permanently delete your profile, usage history, and all associated data within 30 days.
  • Opt out of announcements — reply to any email we send with "unsubscribe" and we will stop sending non-critical messages.

07 Anonymous users

You can use the core token counting and cost estimation features without creating an account. Anonymous usage is subject to IP-based rate limiting. We log the IP address and request metadata for rate limiting and abuse prevention purposes only. We do not link anonymous requests to any persistent identity.

08 Changes to this policy

During the beta period, this policy may change. If we make material changes — particularly changes that affect how we store or share your content or personal data — we will notify registered users by email at least 7 days before the change takes effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

09 Contact

Questions, data requests, or deletion requests: privacy@tokenia.live
